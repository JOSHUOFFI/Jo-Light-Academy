About Us

At Jo-Light Digital Academy We are passionate Tech Educator, Web Developer, and Digital Skills Mentor dedicated to empowering young minds and schools with practical IT knowledge. Through interactive, project-based learning, we help students turn ideas into real-world tech solutions.

Over the years, we’ve trained hundreds of learners in Web Development, Graphics Design, Arduino Programming, and Robotics — blending creativity with innovation. Our mission is to make technology education accessible, hands-on, and fun for everyone.
